Currently I am fulfilling my military duty as a researcher at the Big Data Research Center(Republic of Korea Army). My military service ends on January 2023.

I received my Ph.D. degree (ECE, Electrical and Computer Engineering) at the University of Wisconsin Madison in 2021. I was part of Computational Optics Group lead by Professor Andreas Velten. My research interest lies within computational imaging, signal processing, optical engineering and machine learning. I developed computational algorithms and designed optical imaging systems that can "see around corners". I enjoy prototyping imaging systems and bringing them into "real-world". My representive works are published in Nature and Nature Communications.
